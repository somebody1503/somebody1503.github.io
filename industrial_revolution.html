The direction of the 5th industrial revolution based on the characteristics of the 4th industrial revolution

-4차산업혁명의 특징을 바탕으로 예측해본 5차산업혁명의 방향-


 First of all, the industrial revolution refers to the appearance of changes in the social structure through technological innovation. The characteristics of industrial revolutions appeared centered on machines, it can be said that ‘the 2nd industrial revolution: mass production through the invention of machines, the 3rd industrial revolution: information and communication technology innovation, the 4th industrial revolution: hyperconnectivity’, superintelligence.

 In industrial revolutions, Let's find out about 4th industrial revolution. First, Artificial Intelligence will be an efficient tool for interpreting and analyzing vast amounts of data. Second, Robotics&Drones&Vehicle Automation will make our life confident by doing something dangerous for humans to do and providing more fast movement. Third, the As-A-Service Revolution which Delivers the services you need to live and work on the basis of cloud services will make efficient life through such technology like Intelligent Transportation System which analyze traffic condition and control. Fourth, 5G and enhanced connecticity will make more fast internet service possible. Fifth, Extended Reality which includes virtual reality and augmented reality can perform tasks that are difficult to perform in reality.

 In the 4th industrial revolution, I think improving the capabilities of machines such as AI and 5G was central. Therefore, in the 5th industrial revolution, I think the development through the coexistence of machines and humans will become the center and the proportion of human participation in machines and digital elements will be increased. In addition, as the importance of environmental protection emerges, I think energy innovation through the development of nuclear fusion energy will be achieved.
